Data Deletion Instructions

If you want to delete your data associated with SocialMediaAgent, follow the steps below:

Option 1: Facebook Settings

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings
  2. Navigate to "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find "SocialMediaAgent"
  4. Remove access

This will automatically revoke tokens and trigger data deletion.

Option 2: Direct Request

You can request data deletion by sending an email to:

carlos.fiod@outlook.com

Please include your Facebook User ID or email associated with your account.

Processing Time

All data deletion requests are processed within 7 days.

Data Deleted

  • Access tokens
  • User IDs
  • Associated account and page data

Confirmation

Once your data is deleted, confirmation may be provided upon request.
